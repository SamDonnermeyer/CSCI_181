Ultramarathon Camp 2021!

November 10th-14th, 2021

Overview:

Do you want to grow in your endurance running ability? Then the Missoula Ultrarunning Camp is for you!

No matter what ultra distance you are aiming for, here in Missoula, there are many beautiful and challenging trails that we can train on during the course of the camp. The training includes some lecture style instruction from our guest instructors: Courtney Dauwalter and Gary Robbins, as well as practical instruction â€“ out on the trail! Come and join us for an amazing 5 days where we will train, learn, and grow together!


Camp Contents:

Throughout the course of the camp, you will be able to learn:

  • Nutrition & Gear
  • Training Load
  • Training Design & Execution
  • Succeeding on Race Day

All these areas you will be able to learn about and practically test out the methods during the course of the camp.
